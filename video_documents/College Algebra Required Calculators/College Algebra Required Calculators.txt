These are the required calculators for College Algebra. You will need to have one of the following:
TI-83 Plus
TI-84 Plus
TI-84 Plus Silver Edition
TI-84 Plus CE
We are going to use a lot of features of these calculators in this class.
You can get them used at reasonable prices.
They are also common in high school, so you may be able to borrow one.
A cell phone calculator is not appropriate for this class.
