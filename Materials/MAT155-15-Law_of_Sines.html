Fact - Law of Sines


Given a triangle with angles and opposite sides labeled as the triangle to the right, the ratio of the measurement of an angle to the length of its opposite side will be equal to the other two ratios of angle measure to opposite side. All proportions will be equal.

\[\frac{\sin\alpha}{a} = \frac{\sin\beta}{b} = \frac{sin\gamma}{c}\]
