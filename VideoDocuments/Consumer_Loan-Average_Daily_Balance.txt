# Example

During the month of April, with 30 days, you start with a $430 balance.
Your account has the following activity:

* April 4 --- $58 charge
* April 19 --- $350 payment
* April 22 --- $177 charge
* April 25 --- $49 charge

Use the average daily balance method to calculate the finance charge.
