Book Swap Simulation

This is a simulation of the Riddler Express for December 4, 2020. I'm pretty confident in the answer that I did quickly in my head. Experience tells me that with this level of confidence I need to test my answer.

My original guess is that the probability would be \(\frac{1}{N}\) with \(N\) players.

Since my initial guess was wildly wrong, I decided to look on the Wikipedia page for derangements. The answer to the problem, and the reason why the probability reaches a limiting value can be found there.

The solution to the Riddler Express is below. \[\frac{!5}{5!} = \frac{44}{120} = \frac{11}{30} = 0.36667\]
